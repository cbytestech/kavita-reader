Privacy Policy for KavitaReader

Last updated: December 1, 2024

KavitaReader ("we", "our", or "us") operates the KavitaReader mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

TL;DR: KavitaReader does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to us or third parties. All data stays on your device and your self-hosted Kavita server.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

KavitaReader is designed with privacy in mind. We do not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Track your reading habits or behavior
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Share any data with third parties
  • Display advertisements
  • Store your data on our servers (we don't have any!)

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following information only on your device:

  • Server Connection Details: Your Kavita server URL and authentication credentials
  • App Preferences: Settings like dark mode, font size, and other customization options
  • Reading Progress: Temporary cache of your current reading position

This data is stored using your device's secure encrypted storage and never leaves your device except to communicate with your own Kavita server.

3. Communication with Your Kavita Server

KavitaReader connects directly to your self-hosted Kavita server to:

  • Authenticate your account
  • Retrieve your library content
  • Sync your reading progress
  • Download books for reading

All communication happens directly between the App and your server. We never have access to this data or act as an intermediary.

4. Required Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required to connect to your Kavita server
  • Network State: To detect connectivity and provide appropriate error messages
  • Audio: For optional page-turn sound effects

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and not for data collection.

5. Third-Party Services

KavitaReader does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics providers, or advertising networks. The App is completely self-contained.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The content accessible through the App is determined entirely by your Kavita server's library.

7. Data Security

Your server credentials are stored securely on your device using encrypted storage provided by your operating system. We recommend:

  • Using strong passwords for your Kavita server
  • Keeping your device secure with a lock screen
  • Only connecting to trusted Kavita servers

8. Data Deletion

You can delete all data stored by the App at any time by:

  • Logging out from the App's settings
  • Uninstalling the App from your device
  • Clearing the App's data through your device's settings

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Open Source

KavitaReader is built with transparency in mind. You can verify that we don't collect data by reviewing the source code or through independent security audits.

11. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no data for us to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over all data stored on your device.
