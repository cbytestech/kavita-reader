# Privacy Policy (Legal / Professional Version)

Effective Date: 12/11/2025
Last Updated: 12/11/2025

Kavita Reader (“the App”) is developed and maintained by CookieBytes Technologies (“we”, “us”, “our”). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use the Kavita Reader mobile application and related services.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect
1.1 Information You Provide Directly

The App may store the following information only on your device:

Kavita server URLs

API tokens or credentials used to authenticate with your Kavita server

Reading progress and library metadata

Local settings (theme, preferences, downloaded books)

We do not receive, store, or process this information on our servers.

1.2 Authentication With Google or Apple (Optional)

If you choose to back up app settings through:

Google Drive

Apple iCloud

then data is handled by those platforms according to their privacy policies.
We do not access or store any Google or Apple account information.

The App uses a Google API token provided by CookieBytes solely to enable Drive functionality. This token does not allow us to access your files or credentials.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

The App:

does not collect analytics

does not collect usage data

does not collect device identifiers

does not run advertisements or tracking SDKs

No information is transmitted to CookieBytes or any third party.

2. How Information Is Used

All data stored by the App is used locally on your device only, for purposes including:

Connecting to your self-hosted Kavita server

Managing your library

Saving reading progress

Customizing the reading experience

None of this data is sent to us.

3. How Information Is Shared

We do not sell, rent, or share user data.

The App shares no information with CookieBytes, advertisers, analytics providers, or third parties.

If you choose to connect to your own Kavita server, information is transmitted directly between your device and your server, according to your server’s configuration.

4. Storage and Security

All data is stored:

Locally on your device

Within your Google Drive/iCloud account (if you choose to enable backups)

We do not have access to or control over your storage accounts.

You are responsible for managing access to:

Your device

Your Kavita server

Any cloud services you choose to use

5. Social Features (Future Implementation)

In the future, optional features such as shared reading lists, profiles, or community functionality may be offered.

If implemented:

User profile data may be stored on cookiebytestech.com

Participation will be optional

A revised Privacy Policy will be issued before any such features go live

6. Third-Party Services

The App communicates only with:

Your self-hosted Kavita server

Google Drive or iCloud, if you manually enable backups

We do not integrate any advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

7. Open Source Notice

https://github.com/cbytestech/kavita-reader

Users and contributors may inspect the source code to verify how information is handled.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under the age of 13.
We intentionally collect no data from any user, regardless of age.
